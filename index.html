Game goal

Find the most cats within the Fop22 building within a time limit

Rules

  • When you find a cat, take a photo and share in the whatsapp channel
  • When you take the picture, make sure you mask its location from the other players, by shooting as little as possible (or no) surrounding area
  • When you feel someone has been left out of the competition, PM me the contact details and I will add him/her to the group
  • You can only post every cat once
  • You can only post official game cats. So, no posting your own cat pictures! Official game cats are watermarked so cheaters will be found and dealt with accordingly :p
  • You have to have access to Fop22 to participate

The game can end 2 ways

  1. Time limit. The game ends Friday 22nd of November at 15:00. A final count will be done by our team of judges. Results will be in around 17:00. After which we will celebrate with drinks at a location to be further specified.
  2. Sudden death. When a player has collected all cats in the game he/she wins. The game is over. However, I don’t see this happening any time soon; it took Florian about a month to find the cat I hid near his work area :p

Additional information

  • Toilets are off limits
  • During the game, some hints may be dropped in the whatsapp channel so be sure to follow it closely
  • In case the game ends in sudden death, there will also be drinks on Friday the 22nd of November!

That’s it! Have fun in this exciting game
